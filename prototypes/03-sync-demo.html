Two-device sync demo

Open this same URL on a second device — for example your iPad and your Windows desktop.

Both devices should use the same room name. The default room is quill-demo — change it if you want privacy.

Strokes appear on the other device as you draw, not just after pen-up. Strokes persist locally too — they're still here after a reload, even without the server.
